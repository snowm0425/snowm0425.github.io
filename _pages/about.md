Yu-Hui Huang (黃郁惠) is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Central University (NCU). Prior to joining NCU, she served as an Assistant Professor at Yuan Ze University. Dr. Huang earned her Ph.D. from <a href="https://www.kuleuven.be/english/">KU Leuven</a> (Belgium) and her M.Sc. from <a href="https://www.rwth-aachen.de/cms/~a/root/lidx/1/">RWTH Aachen University</a> (Germany). Her research broadly encompasses computer vision and machine learning. Specifically, she focuses on scene understanding, optical music recognition, and image retrieval, alongside recent explorations into model quantization, multimodal 3D generation, and digital human applications.
